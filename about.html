About the project

What is The Scratcher Project?

The Scratcher Project is a personal startup focused on building practical tools and frameworks for people who make things — designers, developers, writers, and anyone else who spends their days turning ideas into reality.

The idea behind the name

"Scratching the surface" usually means you've barely started. We think that's exactly the right place to be: curious, exploring, not yet locked into a single answer. The Scratcher Project is built around that spirit of early-stage thinking — the phase where possibilities are still wide open.

What we're building

We're starting small and iterating fast. Our focus is on tools that reduce friction in the creative process — things that let you spend more time doing and less time wrangling with your environment.
